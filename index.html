Hatzalah of Houston Launches 2026 Hatzalahthon Fundraising Campaign

Houston's volunteer emergency medical service is calling on the community to support its annual Hatzalahthon campaign. With the motto "1 Community, 1 Heart," Hatzalah of Houston operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year — at absolutely no cost to those they serve. Every dollar raised goes directly toward life-saving equipment, training, and operations. The campaign runs now through the end of the month. Donate at hatzalahthon.com/houston.
