A Hard-Fought Matchup on the Court

May 31, 2026 · By HJI Staff
HPD came away with a 46–38 victory over Mesivta in a hard-fought battle on the court. Compared to last year's matchup, points were much harder to come by, with both teams bringing strong defensive intensity. Mesivta made HPD earn every basket, and HPD responded by stepping up when it mattered most to secure the win. Great effort from both teams and an exciting game for everyone in attendance.
