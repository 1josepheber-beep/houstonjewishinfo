Chabad Beis Din May Review Rising Sheitel Costs

July 16, 2026

Growing concerns over the increasing cost of sheitels have prompted a new initiative calling for rabbinical oversight of pricing.

The effort is reportedly being led by attorney Nechama Tzivin, who plans to bring the issue before the Chabad Beis Din in Israel. Supporters of the initiative argue that the sharp rise in prices has placed a significant financial strain on many families, with some premium sheitels reportedly selling for tens of thousands of shekels.

According to those involved, similar requests may also be submitted to other batei din representing Litvish and Chassidic communities in hopes of encouraging a broader discussion on fair pricing.

The proposal asks rabbinical courts to examine current pricing practices and consider whether any guidance or oversight is appropriate to help address the growing cost of sheitels.

At the time of publication, no public response has been issued by representatives of the sheitel industry regarding the initiative.
