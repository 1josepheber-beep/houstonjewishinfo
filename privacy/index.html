🔒 Privacy Policy

How Houston Jewish Info handles information when you visit.

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how houstonjewishinfo.com (the "Site") handles information when you visit. We are committed to protecting your privacy and collecting only the minimal information needed to operate and improve the Site.

2. Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit personal information to read the Site. We collect limited information automatically through analytics tools, including:

  • Usage data: pages visited, time spent on pages, referring website, and similar browsing activity.
  • Device and technical data: browser type, device type, operating system, and general (city/region-level) geographic location inferred from your IP address.

We do not knowingly collect names, email addresses, payment information, or other directly identifying personal information through the Site.

3. Cookies and Analytics

The Site uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to help us understand how visitors use the Site. The information generated about your use of the Site is transmitted to and stored by Google.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

For more on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. How We Use Information

We use the automatically collected information to:

  • Understand how visitors interact with the Site;
  • Monitor and improve content, performance, and usability;
  • Maintain the security and proper functioning of the Site.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share information with third parties except:

  • With service providers such as Google Analytics that process data on our behalf;
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained according to the default retention settings of our analytics provider. We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us so we can address it.

8. Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable or delete cookies through your browser settings;
  • Opt out of Google Analytics using the tool linked above;
  • Contact us with any questions about your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review it periodically.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@houstonjewishinfo.com.
